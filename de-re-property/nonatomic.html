Reference: nonatomic

Types all
Signature Letter ‘N’
Portability all
Default YES 
@property( nonatomic) id propertyName;

nonatomic is “fluff” in mulle-objc, since all attributes are non-atomic. Atomicity in mulle-objc is either handled by locking (see MulleObject) or through atomic types.
